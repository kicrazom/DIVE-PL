# Related work: component -> nearest precedent -> our difference

Each component of DIVE-PL has prior art. The novelty is the **integration of all
four + a full open-science reproducibility layer**, demonstrated on a real
prospective human survey study. Literature scan: mid-2026 (preprints flagged).

| # | Component | Nearest published precedent | Already established | Our difference |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | LLM **authors** instrument | Current Psychology 2025; *Eur. J. Psychol. Assessment* 2024; *PLOS ONE* 2026 (4-agent GPT-4o); *J. Patient-Reported Outcomes* 2025 | LLM-generated scales reproduce factor structure & reliability of gold-standard instruments | Authoring embedded in an EDC + reviewer + dashboard + open-science chain |
| 2 | **Second LLM as reviewer** (LLM-as-judge) | arXiv:2501.05985 (2025); ChatGPTest arXiv:2405.06329 | Generator+reviewer loop and LLM critique of item wording exist | Reviewer used as a formal, logged QC gate feeding registration-grade instruments |
| 3 | **REDCap + LLM** | Kaiyrbekov et al., *JAMIA Open* 2025; Vanderbilt/Stanford external modules (grey lit) | LLM+REDCap data capture works; AI-built REDCap instruments mostly grey lit | AI-assisted *construction* of registration-ready REDCap instruments, documented & reproducible |
| 4 | **AI-built dashboards** | *Informatics (MDPI)* 2026; REDCap+dashboard+chatbot arch PMC12099335 (2025) | Generative-AI ETL/visualisation as methodology exists | Dashboard published openly (GitHub Pages) as part of the reproducibility layer |
| — | **Conceptual-only AI survey framework** | *Frontiers in Digital Health* 2025 | A conceptual framework exists | We provide an *implemented*, end-to-end, reproducible pipeline + real case study |

## One-line verdict

Every brick is published; the **building** is not. Position DIVE-PL as a
methods/framework paper with an implementation case study, citing precedents honestly.
