AI-usage disclosure

Transparency about AI assistance is a default policy for this project. Generative-AI tools (Claude / Claude Cowork as the design/orchestration agent; GPT as the LLM-as-judge reviewer) were used under human oversight. All study design decisions, scientific claims, and final wording are the responsibility of the authors. Consistent with COPE and ICMJE guidance, AI tools are documented as research aids, not co-authors. Full detail: AI_USAGE_DISCLOSURE.md.
